Our Heritage

A Legacy Built
on Excellence

Founded in 1985 by the Aurum family, our hotel was born from a singular vision: to create a sanctuary where luxury is not a feature, but a way of life. Over three decades and 38 years later, that vision remains our compass.

Nestled in the heart of Hyderabad, Aurum Grand is a landmark of architectural beauty and culinary prestige. We have welcomed heads of state, international celebrities, and discerning travellers who seek nothing short of perfection.

  • 1985
    Founded by Rajiv Aurum with 40 rooms and a dream.
  • 1995
    Expanded to 156 rooms; awarded India's Best Boutique Hotel.
  • 2005
    Opened the Aurum Spa & Wellness Centre and Sky Lounge.
  • 2015
    Received the Presidential Suite floor and Infinity Pool.
  • 2024
    Celebrating 38 years as India's premier luxury destination.
