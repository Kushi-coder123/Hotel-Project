Curated Experiences

Beyond your room, Aurum Grand offers a world of bespoke experiences designed to refresh mind, body, and spirit.

Infinity Pool
Swim in our rooftop infinity pool with panoramic city views, heated year-round.
Aurum Spa
Indulge in world-class treatments inspired by ancient healing traditions and modern wellness.
Fine Dining
